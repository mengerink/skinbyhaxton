Biography

RICHARD HAXTON is an internationally recognized expert in non-surgical rejuvenation, acclaimed for his extraordinary abilities and innovative techniques. With a diverse background in dermatology, herbology, vitamin therapy, cosmetic chemistry, endermology, nutrition, acupuncture, acupressure, ethnic skin care, and advanced Asian and European anti-aging facial methods, he brings a uniquely holistic approach to beauty and wellness.

His studies have also encompassed behavioral therapy for stress-related skin conditions, along with specialized training under leading medical doctors. HAXTON has trained at the Integrated Medicine Center, The Dermal Institute, and numerous other advanced educational programs.

A former professional model, HAXTON's pursuit of skin health began as a personal mission. Frustrated by ineffective products, facials, and traditional medical treatments—and after spending thousands of dollars without results—he set out to develop solutions for himself and others. His breakthroughs not only transformed his own skin but also launched a lifelong dedication to advancing the field of natural rejuvenation.

His methods and results have been featured on television and in print media worldwide, earning him a reputation as a pioneer in anti-aging, beauty, and holistic wellness.

HAXTON's success in reversing his own acne scars and discoloration astounded medical professionals, who confirmed a 95% improvement—far surpassing the 40% improvement plastic surgeons said could be achieved through $50,000 in surgery.
