About Us



Leading skincare specialist, Richard Haxton, joins the team of professionals at Wings of Luv Center for Wellness. Well known for his unique approach to skincare and extensive educational background, Richard has been highly sought after by television and print media to offer his expertise on anti-aging and related skin care issues. Richard has studied a number of disciplines that have contributed to his success in slowing down the effects and appearance of aging. As a former professional model, Richard was inspired by his inability to find skincare products that addressed his individual needs. His latest innovative techniques come from his own personal experiences and those of his exclusive clientele.
